Tiger Woods: A Legend in Golf

Tiger Woods after winning the 2019 Masters, one of the greatest comebacks in sports history.

Eldrick Tont "Tiger" Woods is one of the most accomplished and famous professional golfers of all time. He has won 15 major championships and 82 PGA Tour events, tied for the most in history. Known for revolutionizing the game with his power, precision, and mental toughness, Tiger became a global icon not just in golf but in all of sports. Despite injuries and setbacks, his 2019 Masters victory marked a historic return to the top.
